LUCIFER

Lucifer is a popular TV series based on the DC Comics character Lucifer Morningstar, created by Neil Gaiman, Sam Kieth, and Mike Dringenberg. The show follows Lucifer, the Devil himself, who abandons Hell and moves to Los Angeles to run a nightclub called Lux.



DARK

Dark is a German sci-fi thriller series on Netflix, created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. It’s known for its mind-bending time travel plot, complex character relationships, and deep philosophical themes.



PEAKY BLINDERS

Peaky Blinders is a British crime drama series created by Steven Knight. It follows the story of the Shelby crime family, led by the cunning and ambitious Thomas Shelby (played by Cillian Murphy), in post-World War I Birmingham. The show is known for its intense storytelling, stylish cinematography, and strong performances.




The Umbrella Academy

The Umbrella Academy is a popular Netflix series based on the comic books written by Gerard Way and illustrated by Gabriel Bá. It follows a dysfunctional family of adopted superhero siblings who reunite to solve the mystery of their father’s death and prevent global catastrophes. The show is known for its quirky characters, time-travel elements, and dark humor.
